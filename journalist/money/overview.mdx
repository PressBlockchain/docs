## The Publish Fee Routing

Every Capsule submission costs 30 PRESS. Here's where it goes:

| Recipient | Share | Amount (30 PRESS) |
|---|---|---|
| Validators | 35% | 10.5 PRESS |
| Burn Pool | 20% | 6.0 PRESS |
| Media Validators | 15% | 4.5 PRESS |
| Integrity Treasury | 10% | 3.0 PRESS |
| Vault Operators | 10% | 3.0 PRESS |
| Foundation | 10% | 3.0 PRESS |

This routing is deterministic and on-chain. Nothing is discretionary.

## Setting Up Revenue

Portal → **Monetization**:

1. **Rights** - enable licensing, choose default template, set prices
2. **Ads** - enable ad marketplace, set escrow rules, configure content restrictions
3. **Settlement Reports** - view all revenue, per-contributor payouts, on-chain transaction links

Everything settles automatically. No invoices. No payment delays. No platform middleman.

## Revenue Example - One Investigative Article

| Event | Revenue |
|---|---|
| Capsule submitted | −30 PRESS (fee) |
| Accepted, distribution active | Day 3 |
| 2 license purchases @ 400 PRESS | +776 PRESS net |
| Ad campaign for 30 days | +950 PRESS net |
| **First month net** | **~1,696 PRESS** |

Split between author (70%) and editor (30%) automatically.
