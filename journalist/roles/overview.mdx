## The Role System

Roles exist to balance two needs: **openness** (anyone should be able to participate) and **accountability** (meaningful actions should carry economic weight). The solution: Citizen is free. Higher roles require a bond.

## Role Guide

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Citizen - Free, no commitment needed" icon="user">
    Citizen is free. No bond. No application. Install PressKey and you're a Citizen.

    **What you can do:** Vote on Capsules. Build reputation. File disputes (with a 10 PRESS bond). Read everything.

    **Voting weight:** 1×
    **Publishing:** Not available
    **Who it's for:** Readers, community members, anyone who wants to participate without financial commitment.

    *Marcus votes on 5 articles per week as a Citizen, building his reputation toward Contributor status before he bonds for his Reporter role.*
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Contributor - More weight, fewer limits" icon="user-plus">
    Contributor is a low-bond role for active voters who want more influence.

    **What you get:** 2× base vote weight, higher daily vote limits, fewer throttles.
    **Publishing:** Not available
    **Who it's for:** Active community members who vote frequently and want their votes to count more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reporter - The publishing role" icon="pen">
    Reporter is the core role for working journalists. It unlocks Capsule submission.

    **What you get:** 3× base vote weight, publishing privileges, standard distribution tier.
    **Bond required:** Medium (query Portal for current amount)
    **Who it's for:** Freelance journalists, staff reporters, anyone publishing journalism as Capsules.

    *Maya Chen is a Reporter at The Bay Tribune. Her bond is 50,000 PRESS - economic backing for the accountability behind her investigative work.*
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Verified Author - Maximum weight, premium distribution" icon="star">
    Verified Author is for established journalists with a track record.

    **What you get:** 4× base vote weight, premium distribution tier, reduced court appeal costs.
    **Bond required:** High
    **Who it's for:** Senior journalists, investigative reporters, editors with substantial publishing history.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Media Validator - Powers the integrity layer" icon="shield-halved">
    Media Validators run anomaly detection on published Capsules. They are paid 15% of every publish fee.

    **Duties:** Run integrity algorithms, escalate anomalies to court when warranted.
    **Bond required:** Medium-high
    **Who it's for:** News organizations running technical integrity infrastructure.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Upgrade Path

```
Citizen (free)
    ↓ bond PRESS
Contributor (low bond)
    ↓ bond more PRESS
Reporter (medium bond, publishing unlocked)
    ↓ bond more + build track record
Verified Author (high bond, premium distribution)
```

You can bond directly to any role - you don't have to go in sequence. Many journalists bond directly to Reporter from Citizen.

## How to Get a Role

Portal → **Roles** → **Purchase Role** → Select role → PressKey approves bond deposit → Role activates on-chain confirmation.

Or ask your outlet admin - many outlets help journalists set up and fund their role bonds.
