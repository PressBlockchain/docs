## Five Things That Make PressChain Different

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="No Deletion. Ever." icon="ban" color="#002A43">
    Canonical content is permanent. Corrections create revision lineage — not erasure. The public record of journalism stays intact.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Public Acceptance" icon="users" color="#009BF9">
    No outlet can self-approve. Every article needs 60% weighted public approval and 5% quorum to become canonical.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Claim-Level Disputes" icon="gavel" color="#009BF9">
    Challenges go to the on-chain Court engine, targeting specific claims with evidence and bonded jurors. Process, not deletion.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deterministic Revenue" icon="route" color="#002A43">
    Every fee is split on-chain. Every distribution decision is auditable. No platform controls where the money goes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Evidence on Chain" icon="shield-check" color="#009BF9">
    Source documents are hash-committed on-chain at publication. Tampering is provable. Sources can't be denied.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Rights Marketplace" icon="copyright" color="#002A43">
    Accepted articles can be licensed commercially. Revenue splits between contributors automatically, on-chain, instantly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
