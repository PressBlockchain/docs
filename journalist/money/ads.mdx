## Not Your Average Ad Network

The PressChain ad marketplace is built around **proof-of-placement and escrow** - not click-farm metrics, not opaque algorithms, not platform-extracted revenue.

When an advertiser buys ad placement on your canonical articles, funds are held in escrow until placement is verified on-chain. Then 95% goes to you automatically.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Advertiser creates a campaign">
    An advertiser submits a campaign at `ads.presschain.io`:
    - Ad content and targeting (topic channel, region, minimum outlet tier)
    - PRESS budget (escrowed immediately)
    - Campaign duration
  </Step>
  <Step title="Matching">
    The protocol matches campaigns to your eligible Capsules based on targeting criteria and your distribution tier. Higher tier = better campaign matches.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Placement verified on-chain">
    The SYNC plugin renders ad placements on your article pages. Proof-of-placement is verified automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Settlement">
    95% of the ad spend routes to your outlet wallet - split between contributors per their `shareBps`. 5% protocol fee splits to validators, burn, and foundation.

    Settlement is automatic. No waiting for a check.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enabling Ads

Portal → **Monetization** → **Ads** → Toggle ON

Configure:
- **Minimum escrow period** - how long funds are held before release (e.g., 7 days)
- **Dispute window** - how long advertisers can flag a placement issue
- **Content restrictions** - categories of ads you won't accept

## Revenue Split

```
Ad Campaign Spend: 1,000 PRESS
├── 5% Protocol Fee (50 PRESS)
└── 95% to outlet (950 PRESS)
      → Split by contributor shareBps:
         Author  (7000 bps): 665 PRESS
         Editor  (3000 bps): 285 PRESS
```

## Ad Disputes

If an advertiser disputes a placement (e.g., placement wasn't shown as agreed), the escrowed funds are held pending resolution through the protocol's escrow dispute mechanism. No human arbitrator - deterministic resolution.

## What Makes Your Articles Attractive to Advertisers

- **Premium distribution tier** - your articles reach more readers
- **Verified or Clean integrity label** - advertisers pay more for credible placement
- **Strong acceptance rate** - signals audience engagement
- **Topic channel presence** - advertisers target specific topics

An investigative article with a Verified label in the investigative topic channel will attract better-paying campaigns than an unverified article with no channel placement.
